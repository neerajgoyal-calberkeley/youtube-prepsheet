What we collect

  • Claude API Key — stored locally on your device only. Sent directly to Anthropic's servers when generating a cheatsheet. Never stored on our servers.
  • Video transcript, title & channel name — sent to Anthropic's Claude API to generate your cheatsheet. Not stored by us beyond what Anthropic retains per their own policy.
  • Cheatsheets — stored locally on your device using Chrome's storage. Never uploaded to our servers.
  • Email address & user ID — collected only if you create an account, stored in Google Firebase solely to verify your subscription status.

What we don't collect

  • We do not collect your browsing history
  • We do not track which YouTube videos you watch beyond the one you're actively cheatsheet-ing
  • We do not sell or share your data with advertisers or data brokers
  • We do not store payment information — all payments are handled by Stripe

Third-party services

  • Anthropic — receives your video transcript to generate cheatsheets. See anthropic.com/privacy.
  • Google Firebase — used for account authentication and subscription verification. See Google's privacy policy.
  • Stripe — handles subscription payments. We never see your card details. See stripe.com/privacy.

Data deletion
